About NewEra

The image was the easy part.
We're building the rest.

NewEra is an on-chain AI art platform on BNB Chain. You generate images, own them with your wallet, and sell your work, or the prompt behind it, in a creator-owned market. Every meaningful action settles as a real transaction.

Our mission

A creation economy that creators actually own

We want a place where making something with AI also means owning it, and where the value you create flows back to you instead of into a platform you can't see into. Not rented output on someone's server, but assets that live on a ledger anyone can check.

The problem

Why we're building this

There's a quiet problem with the AI tools everyone uses. You type a prompt, you get an image, and then it lives on someone else's server. The prompt you spent time refining disappears into a text box. The platform shows you numbers you can't verify, behind a subscription you can't question. You made something, but you don't really own anything.

The creation is real, but everything around it is rented. NewEra is our answer to that, when a generation is a transaction, when a sale is a transaction, when ownership actually moves on-chain, the platform stops being a place you visit and becomes a ledger you're part of.

Creation you own

Generate an image and it's tied to your wallet. List it, and if it sells, ownership transfers on-chain and leaves the market. Verifiable, and yours.

Sell the prompt

A good prompt is a skill. On NewEra you can list the prompt behind an image, keep it locked, and buyers unlock it only after they pay.

Proof over hype

Every number on NewEra maps to something that actually happened on-chain. No vanity metrics. Sale splits are enforced by the contract itself.

Under the hood

How a sale works

When someone buys your work, the payment is split automatically by a smart contract, with no middleman touching it. The majority goes straight to you as the creator, a portion supports the platform treasury, and a small slice is burned on every sale. You don't have to trust us to do the math, the contract does it in the open.

What we believe

The principles we build on

Honesty by default

We'd rather under-claim than inflate. If a number isn't real and checkable, it doesn't go on the platform.

Creators come first

The economics are built so the people making the work keep the majority of what it earns.

Open and verifiable

On-chain means anyone can audit what happened. No hidden ledgers, no trust required.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a crypto wallet?
Yes. You need MetaMask (or any compatible Web3 wallet) to connect. Your wallet is your login, with no email or password required.
What is NEA and how do I get it?
NEA is the platform's token (ERC-20 on BNB Chain). New users get a 20 NEA welcome bonus on their first connect, and you earn more by selling your work and through referrals. You use NEA to generate images and trade on the marketplace.
How much does it cost to generate an image?
Each image costs 10 NEA, paid on-chain when you generate.
How do earnings work when I sell?
Every sale is split automatically by a smart contract: 80% goes to you (the creator), 15% to the platform treasury, and 5% is burned. Your share is sent straight to your wallet.
Do I really own my images?
Your generated images are tied to your wallet, and your payment is recorded on-chain. Your claim to each image is backed by a verifiable transaction.
Is this real money right now?
NewEra is currently live on BNB Chain Testnet (testing phase), so it uses test tokens, not real funds.
